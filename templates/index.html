Welcome to EACI Rhema Centre Online Church Services

We do online services on this platform

Services:

Evangelical Assemblies of Christ International is a Bible believing ministry.Our services are Bible centred.In Greek we say "Sola Scriptura".We have Wednesday Online Services for the whole ministry,Tuesday Services for the Youth Group and Friday Services for Men's Fellowship.Women's fellowship meet on Thursdays.Our main Sunday service starts at 9am.

Tuesday Prayers

In all we do in ministry Prayer in the answer.We have our Tuesday Prayers that commences at 5pm up until 6pm in the evening.The intercession group will lead us in prayers.

Wednesay Services

The bible says in Matthew 4v4 "Man shall not live with bread alone...".This being said, as a ministry we have our Wednesday services sharing the bread of life.Group members and commission leaders will share something from 6pm up until 7pm.

Friday Prayers

Like Tuesdays , the ministry also have another prayer day dedicated only for praying for the ministry at large and nothing else.

Praise Team Practices

God needs our praise.Indeed it is very true that when our praises go up His glory comes down.Saturdays starting from 1am the Praise and Worship Team have their practices in preparation for the Sunday Main Service.All Praise and Worship Members are advised to participate.
